Kasey Sanseverino

President/Founder

Kasey is currently a rising senior at UCLA studying Math, Econ and specializing in Computer Science. She is a Westview High School and Black Mountain Middle School alumni and misses living in San Diego. By creating this camp, she hopes to give girls to opportunity to learn about programming to give them an opportunity she never had, the ability to find out early if programming is something they find interesting and want to persue in the future. Her experience includes all of the math and programming classes she has taken for her major, as well as her expereience as Facebook intern during the summer of 2015 and 2016. In the future, it is her goal to work on the business side of an established technological company and eventually start her own.
